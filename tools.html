tool services application
taking the guess work out of your supply chain
problem
Our processes for tracking, allocating and purchasing tools to be used on the manufacturing floor are extremely manual which has lead to increased purchasing and storage costs and delays in job completion.
solution
Leverage Data Analytics and Machine Learning to define data driven decisions and make recommendations to Tool Specialists and Supply Chain Analysts.
outcome
Through our application, we were able to help create a “just in time” culture where the tools are ordered and used at the exact times needed which lead to cost savings within the manufacturing environment as well as in the warehouses. Additionally, transparency and communication were increased through a shared platform to view and evaluate tool inventory.
users
20+ Users: Tool Specialists, Supply Chain Analysts, Supply Chain Managers
devices
Office Laptop, Rugged Laptop
