idea kickstarter
crowdsourcing your company's digital transformation
problem
Boeing’s End User Compute(EUC) organization determines what software products are standardized and rolled out to all employees’ computing equipment. Unfortunately, they lacked a mechanism to gather input and feedback around their product offerings in a single place, which has led to a disconnect between users and EUC decision makers.
solution
Leverage the familiarity of a reddit-style idea submission tool to gather feedback and ideas from end users which will allow them to, as a collective voice, share ideas on how their digital experience can be improved with EUC decision makers.
my role
UI/UX Designer: Performed user and market research to define unmet needs in user population and identify consumer-style related solutions. Designed UI/UX and developed front end using HTML, CSS.
outcome
In the first week of beta release, the applications received 100+ idea submissions with 1000+ votes spread throughout the ideas. This gave insight into both issues with current service offerings as well as present new tools that users were interested in bringing into Boeing.
users
